Solar Irradiance

The power per unit area of sunlight cast upon the Earth.  In areas of the US where there is lots of sunlight year round, many homes and businesses harness that free energy.  It is common to build the price of solar panels into the cost of a  new home.

Wind Velocity Map

This wind velocity heat map illustrates the central US wind corridor, and the coastal wind resources.  The wind corridor runs mostly across lightly populated states.  A typical wind generator produces 2-4 MW of power, and cost 2-3 million dollars to construct. From inception to completion is about 3 years, far less than for standard power generation, where it can take decades of planning to builld and decades to decommission.
